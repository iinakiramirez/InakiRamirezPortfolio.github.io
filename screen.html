Outsiders 20/6/2024

Outsiders is a short film documentary that follows two members of Flock, a birds watching collective, that seeks to bring people of colour to nature, in an attempt to reclaim those spaces Both individuals share their experience with Flock and their personal relationship with nature.

Entre Las Rocas 13/6/2023

Entre Las Rocas - is a short film by Spanish director Jesus F. Cruz. He uses experimental VHS tape manipulation, to create a psychedelic landscape, full of colours and glitches, mirroring the narrative of the film. The film is currently under Festival review and not released to the general public. Below is the teaser of the film.

City of None 4/5/2022 - 1/10/2022

City of None follows a single character, floating from place to place in a big city, suffering through the duelling isolation that society can cause. Through the narrative of the film this mysterious character has to confront the might of the city, challenged by surreal nightmares and distant echoes.

Dialogue Recording / Foley / Audio Editing / Sound Design / Music/Mixing
